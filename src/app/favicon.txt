# Favicon Instructions

To complete SEO optimization, add the following favicon files to the /public folder:

Required files:
1. favicon.ico (16x16, 32x32, 48x48)
2. icon-192x192.png (192x192 pixels)
3. icon-512x512.png (512x512 pixels)
4. apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 pixels)

You can use a favicon generator like:
- https://realfavicongenerator.net/
- https://favicon.io/

Recommended design:
- Use your brand logo
- Primary color: #BF1A1A (red theme)
- Ensure good visibility on both light and dark backgrounds

Once created, place all files in the /public folder.
Next.js will automatically serve them from the root path.
